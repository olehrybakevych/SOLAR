Selection of equipment with maximum performance

We select solar panels taking into account the location of your house, the amount of direct and scattered radiation, so that your power plant produces the maximum amount of electricity and pays off as quickly as possible.

Installation of equipment in accordance with GOST

Installation is carried out in strict accordance with GOST, taking into account the law on the "Green Tariff" and the requirements of Oblenergo. At the same time, we take into account that the solar station has the highest possible performance.

Assistance in connecting to the "Green tariff"
